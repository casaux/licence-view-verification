There are two parts of the digital remote pilot licence. The first part is what you see inside your Apple Wallet on your iPhone and contains a QR code that allows someone to validate your licence details as they appear on the CASA system.

The second part is all the details of your remote pilot licence as appears on your paper licence. To access this view, follow the instructions below.

Tap the 3 dots at the top right of your screen

Scroll down through the licence view to see all the details of your licence.

You can add the remote pilot licence to your Apple Wallet below.

Note that by adding the remote pilot licence to your Apple Wallet, you are consenting to your details being confirmed through the QR code on the front of the digital view to anyone you show the digital view to.
